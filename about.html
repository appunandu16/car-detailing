The WaxWorks Philosophy

More than just a wash

WaxWorks began in 2012 out of a singular, obsessive passion for automotive aesthetics. We noticed that high-end vehicles were often subjected to volume-based detailing shops that caused more harm than good to factory finishes.

We set out to create a truly bespoke studio—a climate-controlled, dust-free environment where cars could be restored, corrected, and protected with surgical precision. Today, we are proud to be the premier destination for exotic and luxury car owners in the city.
