What is Ceramic Coating?

Ceramic Coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds with your vehicle's factory paint, creating a layer of protection. This permanent or semi-permanent bond means it doesn't wash away or break down quickly and does not require repeated applications every few months like wax.

Key Benefits

  • Extreme Hydrophobicity: Water and dirt slide right off, making washing incredibly easy.
  • UV Protection: Prevents paint oxidation and fading from harsh sunlight.
  • Chemical Resistance: Forms a shield against acidic bird droppings, tree sap, and harsh chemicals.
  • Intense Gloss: Enhances the reflective properties of your paint, creating a deep 'candy' gloss.

Our Installation Process

1

Deep Cleansing & Decontamination

We strip old waxes and chemically decontaminate the paint to remove embedded iron and tar.

2

Paint Correction

A mandatory step. We machine polish the paint to remove 80-95% of swirls and scratches, perfecting the finish before sealing it.

3

Surface Prep

Wiping down the vehicle with a specialized solvent to remove all polishing oils.

4

Coating Application

The ceramic formula is applied panel by panel, allowed to flash, and leveled off for a streak-free, mirror-like finish.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Ceramic Coating protect from rock chips?

No. Ceramic coatings protect against chemical damage, UV, and minor wash-marring. To protect against rock chips and deep scratches, Paint Protection Film (PPF) is required.

How long does the coating last?

Depending on the package you choose, our ceramic coatings provide robust protection ranging from 2 years up to 7 years when properly maintained.

Do I still need to use wax?

Never again! The ceramic coating entirely replaces traditional wax and sealants. In fact, applying wax over ceramic coating masks its highly hydrophobic properties.
