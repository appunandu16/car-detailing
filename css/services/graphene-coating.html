What is Graphene Coating?

Graphene is considered a "miracle material." It is an allotrope of carbon consisting of a single layer of atoms arranged in a 2D honeycomb lattice. When infused into an advanced ceramic matrix, it creates a coating that is substantially more durable and resilient against heat and water spotting than traditional ceramic coatings.

Key Benefits

  • Anti-Static Properties: Repels dust and micro-particles, keeping your car cleaner for longer.
  • Less Water Spotting: Dissipates heat incredibly fast, preventing water drops from baking into the paint.
  • Extreme Durability: Has a higher tensile strength and flexibility compared to traditional coatings.
  • Silky Smooth Finish: Offers a uniquely slick, high-gloss surface that is highly resistant to abrasion.

Frequently Asked Questions

Graphene vs. Ceramic: Which is better?

Both are excellent, but Graphene introduces better heat dissipation (which means fewer water spots) and higher anti-static properties (which means less dust). It's the premium evolutionary step over standard ceramic.

How often does it need maintenance?

We recommend a standard maintenance wash every 2 weeks and an annual inspection/decontamination wash at our studio to ensure the warranty remains valid and the coating performs optimally.
