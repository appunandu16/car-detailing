What is Full Detailing?

Our Full Car Detailing package is a comprehensive top-to-bottom cleansing of your vehicle. Far superior to a standard car wash, detailing addresses every nook and cranny of both the interior cabin and exterior paintwork, restoring the vehicle back to absolute showroom condition.

Key Benefits

  • Sanitized Interior: Steam cleaning kills 99.9% of bacteria and removes deep-seated odors.
  • Paint Health: Thorough decontamination prevents environmental fallout from causing rust and decay.
  • Preserved Resale Value: A well-detailed car commands a much higher price on the used market.
  • Restored Comfort: Enjoy the feeling and smell of a brand new car every time you step inside.

Our Detailed Process

1

Deep Interior Cleansing

Vacuuming, hot water extraction for carpets, steam cleaning for plastics, and premium conditioning for all leather surfaces.

2

Exterior Foam Wash

A pH-neutral pre-soak foam lifts dirt and grime before a careful two-bucket hand wash.

3

Wheels & Tires

Deep cleaning of wheel barrels, brake caliper faces, and tires, finished with a non-sling satin tire dressing.

4

Premium Sealant Application

Applying a high-quality polymer sealant or silica spray to provide months of durable gloss and UV protection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will this remove scratches from my paint?

A standard full detail includes a chemical decontamination and sealant, which will not remove scratches. If you have swirl marks or scratches, you will need to add a Paint Correction service.

Can you remove pet hair and odors?

Yes, we use specialized tools for pet hair removal and an ozone generator for permanently eliminating odor-causing bacteria. Note: Extreme pet hair may incur an additional fee.
