What is PPF?

Paint Protection Film (PPF), often referred to as a clear bra, is a thermoplastic polyurethane film applied to the painted surfaces of a new or used car. It is the absolute best way to protect your vehicle's factory paint from rock chips, bug splatters, and minor abrasions.

Key Benefits

  • Self-Healing Technology: Minor scratches and swirls disappear with heat from the sun or engine.
  • Invisible Finish: Available in high-gloss or stealth (matte) finishes that enhance your car's look.
  • Stain Resistance: Protects against bird droppings, acid rain, and road tar.
  • 10-Year Warranty: Industry-leading protection against yellowing, cracking, or peeling.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wash my car normally?

Yes, but we recommend waiting 7 days after installation before the first wash. Avoid using high-pressure water near the edges of the film and commercial car washes with stiff brushes.

Does PPF damage the factory paint?

No, it actually preserves and protects your factory clear coat. When it's time to remove the film (years later), your paint will look brand new underneath.

Can you apply to matte or satin paint?

Absolutely! We offer 'Stealth' PPF which is specifically designed to protect and maintain the look of factory matte/satin finishes, or to convert a glossy car into a matte finish.
